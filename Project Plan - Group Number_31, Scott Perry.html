Group Number31

Members:Kai Smith, Scott Perry, James Epong, Akriti Pun

Goals

To promote the Barrier Reef Orchestra with a modern and well designed website that will provide ease of access to a larger audience.

Success Evaluation

To evaluate the success of the website deployment, we will compare membership, social media (follows, likes), form completion and general attendence compare to the previous year.

Target Audience

Our target audience is anyone with a passion for music and community but we must also expand it so that others are will be interested. Aiming for a younger audience will create a new generation that will form the new basis of members for the future.

Content

Content that is relevant to each page and most importantly, to the BRO. Keeping the page design simple yet ellegent with minimal clicking and ease of scanning.
