    • Goals

    To promote the Barrier Reef Orchestra with a modern and well designed website that will provide ease of access to a larger audience.

    Also to get more business, more life time memberships to continue the life of the business and to get more young people involved with music and performing.

    Success Evaluation

    To evaluate the success of the website deployment, we will compare membership, social media (follows, likes), form completion and general attendence compare to the previous year.

    Measure how many people attend events and the addition of more full time members being added to the page as it expands, then compaire to previous years.

    Success could also be measured by evaluating the amount of times the B.R.O is contacted after the website is live, compared to the previous website.

    Target Audience

    Aiming for a younger audience will create a new generation that will form the new basis of members for the future.

    The webpage is designed to appeal to younger adults and teenagers, it will also target people who are interested in; joining the B.R.O, interested in attending concerts and anyone with a passion for music. Mainly targetting musicians, or people interested in orchestral music.

    Content

    The website will contain an about section, which will contain information about the orchestra. A news section will also be included, which will contain upcoming concerts, as well as any other events that are important to the B.R.O. A contact section wil provide visitors with easy accessable information to contact the customer, and a image gallery will provide visitors with visual information about the orchestra.

    Content that is relevant to each page and most importantly, to the BRO. Keeping the page design simple yet ellegent with minimal clicking and ease of scanning
